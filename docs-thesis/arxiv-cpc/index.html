Abstract

We present QSOT (Quantum State Over Time) Compiler v1.2.3, a production-ready framework for automated verification of relativistic quantum channel dynamics. By integrating special relativistic velocity corrections with automated axiom validation, QSOT enables the precise simulation of quantum information degradation in non-inertial frames.

The system implements a five-stage pipeline: (1) State initialization, (2) Lorentz-boosted quantum evolution, (3) Transfer Tensor Method (TTM) for non-Markovianity detection, (4) AI optimization, and (5) Human-readable lab protocol synthesis. Utilizing this framework, we report the discovery of a critical velocity threshold $\beta_c \approx 0.886c$, marking the onset of Relativistic Coherence Sudden Death (RCSD).
